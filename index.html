Daily News

This year's Minecraft Mob Vote consists of three mobs. The Sniffer, Rascal, and Tuff Golem. Starting off with the Sniffer, they are gentle giants known for their horticultural skills. They are the only ones who can find long lost ancient seeds that you can grow into new unique plants! Next, the rascal, the mysterious mob that can be found in the mines. It loves to play hide-and-seek! It's both playful and helpful, giving you hints for where to search for it. It's generous as well, for if you find it three times, it'll give you a reward to help you with your mining. To find the rascal, you don't need to go all the waw to the deep dark, just below Y=0. It may be mischievous, but it won't lead you into a warden's den! Lastly, the Tuff Golem, the statue that moves. When it's a statue, it can hold and display what you have given it, but when it wakes up from it's slumber, it will stroll around and pick up whatever dropped item it comes across if it's not holding one already. It'll be like any other golem, where you first have to craft it, but unlike them, they are completely passive. If you add a wool block to it's construction, it'll wear a matching cloak! Now, which mobe will you vote for in this year's Mob Vote? The Sniffer, Rascal, or Tuff Golem? You have the power to bring one of these mobs to Minecraft.
